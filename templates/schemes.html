🏛️ Government Schemes for Farmers

Important government programs to help Indian farmers

1. PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)

💰 Benefit: Rs. 6,000 per year directly to farmer's bank account in 3 installments.

👤 Who can apply: All small and marginal farmers who own farmland.

📋 Documents needed: Aadhaar card, bank account, land records.

🌐 How to apply: Visit nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) or apply online.

Apply on pmkisan.gov.in →
2. Fasal Bima Yojana (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana)

💰 Benefit: Crop insurance — get compensation if crops are damaged by floods, drought, or pests.

👤 Who can apply: All farmers growing notified crops.

📋 Documents needed: Land records, bank account, Aadhaar card, sowing certificate.

🌐 How to apply: Contact your nearest bank or apply online.

Apply on pmfby.gov.in →
3. Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

💰 Benefit: Easy loan up to Rs. 3 lakh at very low interest rate (4%) for buying seeds, fertilizers, and equipment.

👤 Who can apply: All farmers, sharecroppers, and tenant farmers.

📋 Documents needed: Aadhaar, land records, passport photo.

🌐 How to apply: Visit any nationalized bank or apply online.

Apply via NABARD →
4. Soil Health Card Scheme

💰 Benefit: Free soil testing and a card telling you exactly which nutrients your soil needs.

👤 Who can apply: Every farmer in India.

📋 Documents needed: Aadhaar card and land details.

🌐 How to apply: Contact your local Krishi Vigyan Kendra or apply online.

Apply on soilhealth.dac.gov.in →
5. PM Krishi Sinchai Yojana (Irrigation Scheme)

💰 Benefit: Subsidy on drip irrigation and sprinkler systems — up to 55% cost covered by government.

👤 Who can apply: All farmers who want to improve irrigation on their farm.

📋 Documents needed: Land records, Aadhaar, bank account.

🌐 How to apply: Visit your district agriculture office or apply online.

Apply on pmksy.gov.in →
6. eNAM (National Agriculture Market)

💰 Benefit: Sell your crops online at the best price across India — no middlemen!

👤 Who can apply: All farmers with land records and bank account.

📋 Documents needed: Aadhaar, bank account, land records.

🌐 How to apply: Register online on the eNAM portal.

Register on enam.gov.in →
