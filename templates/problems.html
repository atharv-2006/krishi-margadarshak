🔧 Common Farming Problems & Solutions

Quick solutions to problems faced by farmers every day

🐛 Problem 1: Pest Attack on Crops

😟 Symptoms: Holes in leaves, yellowing plants, insects visible on crops.

✅ Solution:

  • Use Neem oil spray (natural and safe) — mix 5ml neem oil in 1 litre water
  • Introduce natural predators like ladybugs
  • For severe attacks, use recommended pesticides from your agriculture officer
  • Remove and burn heavily infected plants to stop spreading

🛡️ Prevention: Rotate crops every season, keep farm clean of dead plants.

💧 Problem 2: Waterlogging / Too Much Water

😟 Symptoms: Roots rotting, yellow leaves, foul smell from soil, plants wilting.

✅ Solution:

  • Create drainage channels around your field immediately
  • Add sand or organic compost to improve soil drainage
  • Raise your crop beds higher than ground level
  • Stop watering immediately and let soil dry

🛡️ Prevention: Plan proper drainage before planting. Avoid overwatering.

🌵 Problem 3: Drought / Water Shortage

😟 Symptoms: Dry cracked soil, wilting plants, brown leaves, stunted growth.

✅ Solution:

  • Use drip irrigation — delivers water directly to roots, saves 50% water
  • Mulching — cover soil with dry leaves/straw to hold moisture
  • Water plants early morning or evening, not afternoon
  • Choose drought-resistant crop varieties

🛡️ Prevention: Apply for PM Krishi Sinchai Yojana subsidy for drip irrigation.

🍂 Problem 4: Soil Losing Fertility

😟 Symptoms: Crops growing smaller each year, pale green/yellow leaves, low yield.

✅ Solution:

  • Add organic compost or vermicompost to soil
  • Practice crop rotation — grow different crops each season
  • Grow legumes (dal crops) — they add nitrogen back to soil naturally
  • Get free Soil Health Card to know exactly what nutrients are missing

🛡️ Prevention: Never leave soil empty — grow cover crops between seasons.

🍄 Problem 5: Fungal Disease on Plants

😟 Symptoms: White/black powder on leaves, brown spots, rotting fruits.

✅ Solution:

  • Spray mixture of baking soda + water (1 spoon per litre) on affected plants
  • Remove and destroy infected leaves immediately
  • Improve air circulation between plants by proper spacing
  • Use copper-based fungicide in severe cases

🛡️ Prevention: Avoid wetting leaves while watering. Water at soil level only.
